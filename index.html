Biography

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), working with Prof. Yang Liu and Prof. Xiaoli Li (IEEE/AAIA Fellow). Previously, I received my PhD from the iBRAIN Lab at Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA), supervised by Prof. Daoqiang Zhang (IAPR Fellow) in 2025. I received my M.S. in Computer Science & Technology from Beijing Jiaotong University, supervised by Prof. Lihua Ai in 2021. During my PhD, I was also a visiting PhD student at the Institute of Infocomm Research, A*STAR, supervised by Prof. Xiaoli Li (IEEE/AAIA Fellow).

Research interests:

Sensor-based Time Series Analysis Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCI) Domain Adaptation Foundation Models & LLMs Neuro AI
I warmly welcome potential collaborators, research interns, CSC scholarship students, and visiting students interested in Time Series Analysis, BCI, Foundation Models/LLMs, and related areas. Feel free to reach out!
